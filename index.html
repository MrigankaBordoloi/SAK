About the college

NEF College (P.G.), Guwahati

Established in 1997 under the National Education Foundation, NEF College is a post-graduate institution affiliated to Gauhati University and Dibrugarh University, approved by the Department of Higher Education, Govt. of Assam, and by AICTE, New Delhi.

The college teaches from undergraduate through post-graduate level across the faculties of Arts, Commerce, Management, Social Work and Computer Science — with a record of university rank holders and a nationally ranked Social Work department.
