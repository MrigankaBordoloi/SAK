Campus Facilities

On campus

Built for full-time, regular study

Facilities that support both classroom learning and the fieldwork-heavy programmes like B.S.W. and M.S.W.

📚

Library

A reading room and reference collection covering all faculties taught at the college, with periodicals and past examination papers.

💻

Computer laboratories

Dedicated labs for B.C.A. and other computer-applications coursework, with internet access for research and coursework.

🏛️

Seminar hall

A multi-purpose hall used for guest lectures, seminars, orientation and cultural events.

🏸

Sports & recreation

Indoor and outdoor sporting facilities and an active students' sports and cultural committee.

🤝

Fieldwork & placement cell

Coordinates B.S.W./M.S.W. fieldwork placements and final-year placement assistance across departments.

🩺

Student support

Office staff and faculty mentors available for academic, administrative and welfare queries throughout the session.
