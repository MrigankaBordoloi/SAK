Privacy Policy

SAK College respects the privacy of visitors and applicants. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • Contact details submitted through the enquiry form (name, email, phone, course of interest, message)
  • Admission-related documents submitted during the application process

How we use it

Information submitted through this site is used only to respond to enquiries, process admissions, and communicate important notices such as merit lists and fee deadlines. It is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data retention

Enquiry and admission records are retained for as long as required for academic administration and regulatory compliance.

Contact

For questions about this policy or to request removal of your enquiry data, contact the college via the contact page.
