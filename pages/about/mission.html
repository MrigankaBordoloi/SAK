Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable and disciplined higher education across the Arts, Commerce, Management, Social Work and Computer Science, delivered by qualified faculty in a full-time, regular format, so that every enrolled student is equipped for further study, employment or public service.

Our Vision

To be recognised as one of Eastern India's leading post-graduate colleges — known for the quality of its teaching, the integrity of its examinations, and the strength of its Social Work and professional programmes — while remaining true to the National Education Foundation Trust's founding commitment to affordable education.

Core values

  • Access — affordable fees and an instalment facility so cost is never a barrier to a capable student.
  • Discipline — full-time, regular attendance and coursework, not a distance or correspondence model.
  • Community — fieldwork and social-work practice that connects the classroom to Assam's communities.
  • Merit — transparent, merit-based admission and examination processes.
In practice: this mission is what shapes decisions from the fee structure to the choice of majors offered — see the fee structure and courses offered.
