Chairman's Message

It is my privilege to welcome you to SAK College, an institution built on a simple conviction: that quality higher education, delivered with discipline and care, can change the trajectory of a student's life. The National Education Foundation Trust was established to make that conviction real across Assam, and this college is one of its most visible expressions.

Since 1997, we have grown from a modest beginning into a multi-faculty post-graduate college affiliated to both Gauhati University and Dibrugarh University, with programmes spanning the Arts, Commerce, Management, Social Work and Computer Science. Growth, for us, has never been only about numbers — it has been about building departments that produce rank holders, a Social Work programme recognised nationally, and graduates who go on to serve their communities.

I encourage every student who walks through our gates to treat the classroom as the beginning, not the end, of their education. Our faculty, staff and governing body remain committed to giving you the environment, guidance and opportunity to do exactly that.

With good wishes,
Chairman, National Education Foundation Trust

A commitment to access

The Trust keeps fees affordable and offers an instalment facility so that financial constraint is never the reason a capable student is turned away. This remains a founding principle of every institution under the SAK umbrella.
