Principal's Desk

Welcome to SAK College. As Principal, my responsibility is to translate the Trust's vision into the everyday experience of every student on this campus — from the quality of teaching in the classroom to the fairness of an examination, from the availability of a seat in the library to the promptness of an admission enquiry.

We run a full-time, regular college. That is a deliberate choice: our students attend class, engage with faculty, and build the discipline that carries them into further study or a career, rather than treating a degree as a formality. Attendance requirements exist not as a bureaucratic hurdle but because a college education happens in the room, in discussion, and in the relationships between teacher and student.

Our academic departments are staffed by qualified, experienced faculty who mentor students well beyond the syllabus — in research, in competitive-exam preparation, and in the fieldwork that defines programmes like B.S.W. and M.S.W. The results show in our university rank holders and in a Social Work department ranked among the best in India.

Priorities for the current session

  • Timely, transparent admission and merit-list processes across CUET and Non-CUET rounds.
  • Continued strengthening of placement support and industry linkages for professional courses.
  • Investment in library, laboratory and seminar infrastructure.
My door is open: students and parents with concerns about admission, academics or campus life are welcome to reach the Principal's office directly through the college contact page.
