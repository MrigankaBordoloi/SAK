Why affiliation matters

A degree from an affiliated, approved institution is recognised for further study and for employment, including government recruitment and eligibility for entrance examinations that require a UGC/AICTE-recognised qualification. Always confirm a course's specific affiliating university before you apply — see the individual course pages for programme-wise affiliation.
