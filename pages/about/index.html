The College

SAK College, Guwahati was established under the National Education Foundation (SAK) Trust — a charitable educational trust registered under the Indian Trusts Act, 1882 and u/s 12A of the Income Tax Act, 1961. Spreading quality education is a core objective of the Trust, pursued through a family of schools and colleges across Assam.

The college teaches from Higher Secondary through Graduation to Post-Graduate level, in both general and professional streams.

Recognition & affiliation

  • NOC granted by the Govt. of Assam, Education Department (2018) for TDC & PG courses in Arts, Science and Commerce.
  • Permission and affiliation from Gauhati University from session 2018–19.
  • Affiliation from Dibrugarh University for professional courses — B.B.A., B.S.W., M.S.W., M.B.A.
  • Approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi.
  • Permission from the Assam Higher Secondary Education Council for H.S. in Arts and Commerce.
Nationally recognised: the Department of Social Work has featured in India Today's ranking of India's best MSW colleges.
