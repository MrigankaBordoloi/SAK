Master of Commerce (M.Com.)

A 2-year postgraduate programme affiliated to Gauhati University for advanced study in commerce, accounting and finance.

Programme at a glance

Duration2 years (4 semesters)
AffiliationGauhati University
EligibilityB.Com. or equivalent bachelor's degree from a recognised university
ModeFull-time, regular
IntakeAs per university-sanctioned seats; merit-based admission

Areas of advanced study

  • Advanced Financial Accounting · Corporate Tax Planning
  • Financial Management · Research Methodology in Commerce
Attendance: students must attend the stipulated percentage of classes to be eligible for university examinations.

Career pathways

Graduates pursue teaching, research (M.Phil./Ph.D.), professional qualifications (CA/CS/CMA), and careers in accounting, taxation and corporate finance.
