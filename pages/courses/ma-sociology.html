M.A. Sociology

A 2-year postgraduate programme affiliated to Gauhati University, with a strong research foundation in social theory and empirical methods.

Programme at a glance

Duration2 years (4 semesters)
AffiliationGauhati University
EligibilityB.A. (Sociology or related discipline) or equivalent bachelor's degree from a recognised university
ModeFull-time, regular
IntakeAs per university-sanctioned seats; merit-based admission

Areas of advanced study

  • Classical & Contemporary Sociological Theory · Research Methodology
  • Sociology of Assam & the Northeast · Gender & Society
Track record: SAK's B.A. Sociology students have secured university rank positions, including Jennifer Rahman and Urbi Boro (1st and 3rd rank, Gauhati University).

Career pathways

Graduates pursue research (M.Phil./Ph.D.), civil services, teaching, the development sector, and roles in Social Work alongside SAK's M.S.W. programme.
