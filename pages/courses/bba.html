Bachelor of Business Administration (B.B.A.)

A 3-year professional undergraduate programme affiliated to Dibrugarh University, building foundational management skills across finance, marketing, HR and operations.

Programme at a glance

Duration3 years (6 semesters)
AffiliationDibrugarh University
EligibilityHigher Secondary (10+2) pass in any stream from a recognised board
ModeFull-time, regular
IntakeAs per university-sanctioned seats; merit-based admission

Core areas of study

  • Principles of Management · Marketing Management · Financial Management
  • Human Resource Management · Business Communication · Entrepreneurship
Attendance: students must attend the stipulated percentage of classes to be eligible for university examinations.

Career pathways

Graduates move into M.B.A. study (including at SAK), management-trainee roles in corporates and banks, and entrepreneurship.
