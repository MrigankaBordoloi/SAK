Bachelor of Social Work (B.S.W.)

A 3-year undergraduate programme affiliated to Dibrugarh University, combining classroom theory with structured fieldwork in community and social-welfare settings.

Programme at a glance

Duration3 years (6 semesters)
AffiliationDibrugarh University
EligibilityHigher Secondary (10+2) pass in any stream from a recognised board
ModeFull-time, regular, with mandatory fieldwork placements
IntakeAs per university-sanctioned seats; merit-based admission

Core areas of study

  • Social Case Work · Community Organisation · Social Welfare Administration
  • Human Growth & Development · Rural & Urban Community Development
Fieldwork: students complete supervised placements with NGOs, government welfare schemes and community organisations across Assam.

Career pathways

Graduates progress to M.S.W. (offered at NEF), and to roles in NGOs, government welfare departments, corporate social responsibility (CSR) teams and international development organisations.
