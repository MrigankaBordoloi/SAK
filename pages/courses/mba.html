Master of Business Administration (M.B.A.)

A 2-year AICTE-approved postgraduate management programme affiliated to Dibrugarh University, with specialisation options in Finance, Marketing and Human Resource Management.

Programme at a glance

Duration2 years (4 semesters)
AffiliationDibrugarh University · AICTE approved
EligibilityBachelor's degree (any discipline) from a recognised university, minimum aggregate as per university norms
ModeFull-time, regular
IntakeAs per university-sanctioned seats; merit-based admission

Specialisations offered

  • Financial Management · Marketing Management
  • Human Resource Management · Strategic Management
Placement support: the college provides placement assistance and industry interaction for M.B.A. students through its alumni network.

Career pathways

Graduates move into management, banking, marketing and HR roles in the corporate and public sectors, or pursue entrepreneurship.
