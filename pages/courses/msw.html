Master of Social Work (M.S.W.)

A 2-year postgraduate programme affiliated to Dibrugarh University. NEF College's Department of Social Work has featured in India Today's national ranking of India's best MSW colleges.

Programme at a glance

Duration2 years (4 semesters)
AffiliationDibrugarh University
EligibilityBachelor's degree (any discipline, B.S.W. preferred) from a recognised university
ModeFull-time, regular, with advanced fieldwork placements
IntakeAs per university-sanctioned seats; merit-based admission

Specialisations offered

  • Medical & Psychiatric Social Work · Community Development
  • Labour Welfare & Human Resource Management
National ranking: #47 for Social Work in India — India Today. Fieldwork partners include NGOs, hospitals and government welfare programmes.

Career pathways

Graduates take up roles as medical and psychiatric social workers, in NGO leadership, CSR, labour welfare, disaster management and public policy.
