Fee Structure — Session 2026–27

SAK College keeps fees affordable and offers an instalment facility. Figures below are indicative per-semester/per-year tuition; admission, examination and library fees are charged separately as per university norms.

CourseDurationIndicative annual fee
B.A. / B.Com.4 / 3 years₹18,000 – ₹22,000
B.B.A. / B.C.A.3 years₹28,000 – ₹34,000
B.S.W.3 years₹24,000 – ₹28,000
M.B.A.2 years₹45,000 – ₹52,000
M.S.W. / M.Com. / M.A. Sociology2 years₹22,000 – ₹28,000
Instalment facility: fees may be paid in two instalments per academic year on request at the college office. The full, official fee breakdown is issued as a downloadable PDF at the time of admission.

What's included

  • Tuition fee for the full academic year
  • Library and laboratory access fee
  • Examination fee is charged separately by the affiliating university
