Prospectus — Session 2026–27

The college prospectus is the complete reference for prospective students: programmes offered, eligibility, fee structure, campus facilities, code of conduct and academic calendar.

Download: the prospectus PDF is issued each admission season and made available through the college office and admission portal. Contact the college if you need a copy sent to you.

What's inside

  • Programme-wise eligibility, duration and affiliation
  • Fee structure and instalment facility details
  • Campus facilities — library, laboratories, sports, hostel information
  • Rules of conduct, attendance and examination eligibility
  • Academic calendar for the session
