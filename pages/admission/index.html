Admission Procedure — Session 2026–27

Registration is open for admission into all courses. Prospective students may apply online or offline.

  1. Apply online or collect the form

    Fill the admission form through the online portal, or collect and submit a physical form at the college office.

  2. Merit list publication

    Merit lists (CUET and Non-CUET rounds) are published on the website and college notice board.

  3. Document verification

    Report to the college with original mark sheets, certificates, caste/reservation documents (if applicable) and passport photographs.

  4. Fee payment

    Pay admission fees at the counter or via SAKT/online transfer using the published bank details. An instalment facility is available.

  5. Enrolment confirmation

    Receive your enrolment confirmation and class-commencement schedule from the office.

Note: admission to professional courses (B.B.A., M.B.A., B.S.W., M.S.W.) follows Dibrugarh University norms; B.A./B.Com. rounds follow Gauhati University CUET / Non-CUET schedules.

Documents required

  • H.S. / degree mark sheets and pass certificates (original + photocopy)
  • Age-proof certificate (H.S.L.C. admit/certificate)
  • Caste certificate, if claiming reservation
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Migration certificate (for boards/universities outside Assam)
